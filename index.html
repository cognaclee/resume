About me

I received a B.S. degree from Beijing University of Technology (BJUT) in 2015. I am currently working toward a Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Na Lei at Dalian University of Technology. My current research interests include image processing, point cloud processing, and mesh generation.
